Heart Disease Prediction (2021)

  • Created 14 Classification models and trained them on a global dataset of patients with heart disease
  • Models perform with accuracy that matches leading models and sensitivity that outperforms leading models and research
    • Random Forest KPCA with Bagging achieved 94% sensitivity and 85% accuracy
    • Linear SVC model with accuracy and sensitivity of 87%
  • Highlighted the need for more research into data from varying medical centers that collect information that has not been previously used in heart disease research

Face Mask Detection and Recogntion (2021)

  • Created an Image Processing model based on VGGNet, a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN), to classify faces based on the presence of face masks
  • Used modified Haar Cascade logic to find faces in real world images
  • Validation accuracy of 99.15% on input images of faces
  • Locates and classifies real-world faces in a usable interface

FIFA Player Analysis (2021)

  • We used Principal Component Analysis to categorize all player statistics into the fice components of Attacking, Defending, Hype & Value, Players Physique, and Others
  • Exploratory Factor Analysis revealed that player reputation and compensation are not linearly related
  • Canonical Correspondence Analysis confirmed that player's skills and attributes are strongly correlated to their position

Netflix Prize Data (2020)

  • Netflix Prize data of 100,480,507 movie ratings from Netflix users
  • Reduced data usage by 80% through data cleaning, ranking, and pickling
  • K-Means Clustering and K-Nearest Neighbors created user and movie groups to recommend movies to users with reinforcement learning
  • We used Singular Value Decomposition to reduce dimensionality as well as to predict specific ratings on movies by individual users
